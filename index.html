ONE OF US
Global Foundation

The One of Us Global Foundation created in memory of Alexander "Technoblade"; specializes in finding people's voices and dreams. After the community lost Technoblade, his father, known as "Mr. Technodad" or "MrFaceplate" in Minecraft, decided to uphold his son's community by connecting with others, even with the tragic loss. Now, he and his wife, Stacey Street, partnered with the Sarcoma Foundation launching the One of Us Global Foundation. To view more of their work and goals, view their website:
